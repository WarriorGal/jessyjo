Jessy Jo Gomez attended Parsons The New School for Design on a full scholarship, majoring in Design & Technology (concentration: game design) and minoring in Japanese Studies. She graduated in Spring 2016. (woo~hoo!)
✿

Jessy Jo aims to teach kids how to code through game design using programs like Stencyl and MinecraftEdu. She currently teaches two after school classes at four different schools, where her students refer to her as "Ms. Gamez".
✿

Jessy Jo also believes in using the power of games for not only education, but for social justice:

"Saving the world through game design."
